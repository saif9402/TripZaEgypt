Booking & Payments

How can I book a trip?
Select your trip, choose the date and number of guests, click Book Now, fill in your details, and proceed with payment.
What payment methods are available?
cash on spot
Will I receive instant confirmation?
Yes, for trips with available seats. Some experiences require a short review before confirmation.
How will I receive my ticket?
An electronic ticket will be sent to your email and WhatsApp with your booking number.
How do I check availability?
The calendar on each trip page shows real-time availability.

Pricing & Taxes

Are taxes included in the price?
Yes, all prices include Egypt’s 14% VAT unless stated otherwise.
What is included in the trip price?
Transportation, meals, drinks, and entrance fees as listed in the trip details.
Are there hidden fees?
No, the final price is shown before checkout. Any extra charges are clearly displayed.
Are tips included?
Tips are optional unless stated in the trip details.
Do prices differ for Egyptians and foreigners?
Yes, some attractions apply different ticket rates. This is always mentioned in trip details.
Are there child discounts?
Yes, child pricing depends on age and trip type. Prices are shown during booking.

Changes & Cancellations

Can I change my booking date?
Yes, if the trip policy allows it. Changes are usually possible up to 24 hours before departure.
What is the cancellation policy?
Most trips offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure. Some are non-refundable.
What happens if a trip is cancelled due to weather or unforeseen events?
You can choose to reschedule, receive credit, or get a full refund.
What if I don’t show up?
No-shows are considered used bookings and are non-refundable unless stated otherwise.

Logistics & Operations

Is there a minimum number of participants?
Some trips require a minimum number of guests. We will inform you and offer alternatives or a refund if the minimum is not met.
Do you provide hotel pickup?
Yes, in selected areas. Extra charges may apply. Check pickup points before confirming your booking.
What if the bus is delayed?
Traffic may cause slight delays. You will receive live updates with the adjusted meeting point if needed.
What is the luggage policy?
One small backpack per guest is allowed. Larger luggage depends on the trip type.

Requirements & Accessibility

What documents do I need?
Egyptians: National ID.
Foreigners: Passport and booking ticket.
What should I bring?
Valid ID, sun protection, water, and weather-appropriate clothing. Follow safety instructions at all times.
Are the trips accessible for travelers with disabilities?
Yes, some trips are wheelchair accessible. Look for the accessibility icon or contact us for arrangements.
Do I need a security permit?
For some destinations, yes. We will inform you and handle the process once we receive your details in advance.
Which languages are available for guided tours?
Most tours are available in English and Arabic. Other languages may be available depending on the trip.
Do you offer travel insurance?
Insurance is available for selected trips. The option appears during booking.

Support & Extras

How can I contact support?
Live chat, phone, and email support are available daily from 9 AM to 11 PM (Cairo time).
Do you have a loyalty program or discount codes?
Yes, you can earn points with each booking and redeem seasonal promo codes.
Can I book for a company or a large group?
Yes, we provide tailored offers for corporate and group bookings. Contact us for details.
Can I request a tax invoice?
Yes, request it during booking or through customer support after payment.
How can I report an issue?
Submit a support ticket from your account with photos or videos. We respond within 24 hours.
What safety measures do you follow?
All partners are licensed. Vehicles and safety equipment are regularly inspected.
